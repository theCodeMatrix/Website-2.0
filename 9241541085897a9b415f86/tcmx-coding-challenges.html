The works

  • Every Sunday we will be posting a new problem and the winner of that coding challenge will be announced on Saturday. Submissions close on Friday. Parents, make sure that students are trying the problems on their own!  
  • Solutions will be judged on completion and creativity. 
  • You can send your solutions to tcmx.challenges@gmail.com
  • You are welcome to try problems from previous weeks, however only send solutions to the problem of that week, not previous weeks.  
  • The problems are targeted towards students in grades 4-8 (ages 10-14), however anyone is welcome to try the problem and submit solutions.  ​

Prerequisites:
Having some prior coding experience is useful if you want to do Level II TCMX Coding Challenges. 
If you are a beginner, no worries! Try out Level I and email us with any questions you might have. 

Downloading ﻿BlueJ﻿:
BlueJ is a free Java development environment designed for people who are learning how to code. 
Our team handpicked this software, and you shouldn't run into any issues with viruses.
We recommend using this platform to work on the challenges.

Webinars (Web Seminars):
​We will be holding live webinars soon, so if you are looking forward to learning some coding skills, stay posted.
